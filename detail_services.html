Why choose us

Employees receive a link to the IMAGINO® assessment tool via email, which will take them about twenty minutes to complete. The IMAGINO® algorithm calculates the results, which are then confidentially delivered to clients via email. The analysis benchmarks individual results against the golden standard (top 10% results) and against our database averages for 9 individual and 6 organizational drivers of individual creativity and corporate innovation within an organization. These results are presented as a one-page document. Participants will also receive detailed descriptor of the results, which includes behavior descriptions for each driver of individual creativity and organizational innovation climate of the assessment. The analysis will identify any individual creativity and/or organizational innovation gaps, which can then be addressed in a variety of ways including one-on-one coaching, webinars, and/or group training sessions. The tool also discovers who are the creativity champions within the organization. Such creative employees need to be actively involved in creativity and innovation projects within the corporation.

The IMAGINO® concept offers executives, of all levels of seniority within a company, a crucial tool kit to unlock their individual creativity by connecting with their imagination and provoking out-of-the-box thinking. Unlocking the individual creativity of employees and championing innovation from within can turbo-charge corporate innovation across all aspects of a company’s operations from new products and services, to the novel processes.
